Comparison

Both MPCVET and SEPR offer a wide range of vocational courses aimed at providing practical skills and professional training. While MPCVET focuses on areas like automobile engineering, IT, electrical engineering, fashion design, and beauty & wellness, SEPR offers unique courses such as graphic design, culinary arts, and jewelry design, alongside common fields like automobile mechanics and electrical engineering.
